Understanding Step 4

Step 4 involves conducting a thorough and honest self-examination to identify personal flaws, resentments, and behaviors that have contributed to our struggles. This step is crucial for personal growth and lays the foundation for subsequent steps in the recovery process.

Key Concepts

  • Honesty: Being truthful with oneself about past behaviors and their impact.
  • Courage: Facing uncomfortable truths without fear.
  • Self-Reflection: Understanding personal strengths and weaknesses.
  • Responsibility: Acknowledging one's role in past actions and their consequences.
