Fourth Step Inventory Worksheet

Questions to Consider:

  • Who do you resent?
  • What is the cause of your resentment?
  • What is your part in the resentment?
  • What is the underlying fear associated with this resentment?
  • What is the harm caused by this resentment?

Questions to Consider:

  • What are your fears?
  • What is the basis of these fears?
  • How do these fears affect your life?
  • What is the worst thing that could happen if your fear came true?
  • What is your part in these fears?

Questions to Consider:

  • Have you been selfish or inconsiderate in your sexual conduct?
  • Have you been dishonest or manipulative?
  • Have you been exploitative or abusive?
  • Have you been unfaithful or deceitful?
  • What harm have you caused to others through your sexual conduct?

Questions to Consider:

  • Who have you harmed?
  • What was the nature of the harm?
  • What was your motive or justification?
  • What was the impact of your actions on the other person?
  • What is your part in the harm caused?

Questions to Consider:

  • What are your strengths and positive qualities?
  • What virtues do you possess?
  • What talents or skills do you have?
  • What positive actions have you taken?
  • How have you contributed to the well-being of others?
