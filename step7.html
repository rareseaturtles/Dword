Step 7: Humbly Asking for Removal of Shortcomings

Understanding Step 7

Step 7 involves humbly asking our Higher Power to remove our shortcomings. This step emphasizes humility and the acknowledgment that we cannot overcome our character defects solely through our own efforts.

Key Concepts

  • Humility: Recognizing our limitations and seeking assistance beyond ourselves.
  • Acceptance: Acknowledging our flaws without self-condemnation.
  • Faith: Trusting in a Higher Power to facilitate our personal growth.
  • Transformation: Embracing the process of change and self-improvement.

How to Work Step 7

  1. Reflect on Your Shortcomings: Review the character defects identified in previous steps.
  2. Practice Humility: Acknowledge that you need help beyond your own abilities to overcome these shortcomings.
  3. Pray or Meditate: Humbly ask your Higher Power to remove your defects of character.
  4. Seek Support: Discuss your journey with a sponsor or support group to gain insights and encouragement.
  5. Embrace Patience: Understand that change is a gradual process and requires ongoing effort.

Inspirational Quotes

"Humbly asked Him to remove our shortcomings." — Alcoholics Anonymous

"My Creator, I am now willing that you should have all of me, good and bad. I pray that you now remove from me every single defect of character which stands in the way of my usefulness to you and my fellows." — Seventh Step Prayer

Reflection

Consider how humility plays a role in your recovery. Reflect on the importance of seeking help and the freedom that comes from releasing your shortcomings to a Higher Power.
