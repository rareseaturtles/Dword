Step 3: Turning Over Our Will

Understanding Step 3

Step 3 involves making a conscious decision to turn our will and our lives over to the care of a Higher Power, as we understand it. This step signifies a pivotal moment in the recovery journey, where we acknowledge that self-will alone is insufficient and embrace the guidance of a power greater than ourselves.

Key Concepts

  • Surrender: Letting go of self-directed control and trusting in a Higher Power's guidance.
  • Faith: Believing in the benevolence and support of a Higher Power.
  • Commitment: Making a deliberate choice to follow a spiritual path in recovery.

Inspirational Quotes

"Made a decision to turn our will and our lives over to the care of God as we understood Him."

— Alcoholics Anonymous

Reflection
