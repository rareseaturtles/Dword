Step 6: Becoming Ready for Character Transformation

Understanding Step 6

Step 6 involves becoming entirely ready to have our defects of character removed. This step signifies a willingness to let go of negative traits and behaviors that hinder our recovery and personal growth.

Key Concepts

  • Recognition: Identifying personal character defects that impede progress.
  • Willingness: Developing a sincere desire to let go of these defects.
  • Humility: Accepting that change requires openness and acceptance.
  • Readiness: Preparing oneself mentally and spiritually for transformation.

How to Work Step 6

  1. Reflect on Previous Steps: Review your personal inventory and the admissions made in Steps 4 and 5 to identify defects of character.
  2. Acknowledge Each Defect: Clearly recognize and accept the specific defects you wish to change.
  3. Develop Willingness: Cultivate a genuine readiness to let go of these defects, understanding their impact on your life.
  4. Seek Support: Engage with your support network, such as sponsors or support groups, to discuss your readiness and seek guidance.
  5. Practice Patience: Understand that becoming entirely ready is a process that requires time and self-compassion.
