Step 5: Admitted to God, to ourselves, and to another human being the exact nature of our wrongs.

Understanding Step 5

Step 5 involves acknowledging and sharing the exact nature of our wrongs with God, ourselves, and another human being. This step is crucial for personal growth and spiritual development.

Key Concepts

  • Honesty: Being truthful with ourselves and others about our actions and their impacts.
  • Accountability: Taking responsibility for our actions and their consequences.
  • Spiritual Growth: Deepening our relationship with a Higher Power through openness and humility.
  • Healing: Releasing guilt and shame associated with past behaviors.

How to Complete Step 5

  1. Reflect on Your Inventory: Review the moral inventory completed in Step 4.
  2. Choose a Trusted Person: Select someone you trust to share your inventory.
  3. Set a Time and Place: Arrange a private and comfortable setting to discuss your inventory.
  4. Prepare Spiritually: Engage in prayer or meditation to seek guidance and strength.
  5. Share Your Inventory: Openly discuss each item on your list, acknowledging the exact nature of your wrongs.
  6. Receive Feedback: Be open to feedback and support from the person you're sharing with.
